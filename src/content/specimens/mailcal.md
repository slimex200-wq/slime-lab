### What it is

An experiment in extracting calendar events from email — reading messages and turning dates, times, and commitments buried in prose into structured schedule entries.

### The experiment

The question was narrow: how reliably can event extraction work on real inbox mail before any product is built around it? I scoped it as a proof of concept and started with the extraction stage alone, deferring sync and UI until the core was proven.

### Findings

Frozen at PoC stage one in April 2026, before the extraction question got a definitive answer. Nothing here failed — other projects simply took priority. The work is preserved as-is, and the problem still seems worth revisiting when the queue clears.
